About

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor of 19th and 20th Century Anglophone Literature in the Department of English at the University of Iowa. I earned my PhD from the Department of English at the University of Virginia, where I was a Fellow at the Jefferson Scholars Foundation from 2020 to 2025.

I was born and raised in the former British colony of Hong Kong, and also spent my formative years in Vancouver, Canada. Beyond my academic work, I enjoy playing my guitars, swimming, and yoga. I am looking forward to engaging with the artistic and writerly communities that have shaped Iowa City into one of the world's many vibrant places for literary life.
